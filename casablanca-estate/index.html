About This Project

Casablanca is a beautifully finished Smart Homes, located at Road 35, along 3rd Avenue, Gwarimpa District, Abuja. Don’t miss your chance to live in your next dream home in the heart of Gwarimpa. The living area is airy with a feeling of liberation. Attached, there is a large private terrace running through the entire length of the apartment including a kids playground and private Jacuzzi. Overall, the layout of this residence has been well thought out to include spacious bedrooms, walk in wardrobes and stunning en0suite bathrooms.

The garden is particularly impressive with ample garden in front, side and back, making the house well positioned on the plot. The upstairs offers a living family area, a large master and en suite bedroom as well as two other bedrooms sharing a jack and hill bathroom.
